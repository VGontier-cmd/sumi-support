# Privacy Policy — Sumi

**Last updated: 2026-05-03**

This document describes how the Sumi mobile app handles your data.

## Short version

Sumi does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any of your personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

## What data the app processes

The app stores the following information **only on your device**, in its local secure storage:

- Your learning progress (which kana you've seen, which you've mastered, your review history)
- Your streak and achievements
- Your preferences (sound, haptics, notification time, quiz settings, active script)
- A short history of your recent quiz sessions (max 200 sessions, max 100 reviews per kana)

This data is never sent to a server. There is no Sumi backend.

## Third parties

The app uses the following platform services, which receive no Sumi-specific data:

- **Apple iOS / Google Android system text-to-speech** to pronounce kana and words. The text sent to the system TTS is the kana / Japanese word being practised. Your device's operating system handles this — Sumi has no visibility into it.
- **Apple Push Notifications / Android Notifications**, scheduled locally on the device, to remind you to study at your chosen time. No remote push.

The app does **not** integrate any analytics SDK (no Firebase, no Sentry, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel, etc.).

## Permissions the app requests

- **Notifications** (optional) — only to send the daily study reminder you opt into in Settings. You can revoke it at any time.

The app does not request the camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or any other sensitive permission.

## Data retention and deletion

All data lives on your device. To delete it:

- **In-app**: Settings → "Recommencer l'apprentissage" (clears progress, keeps achievements) or "Tout effacer" (deletes everything).
- **From the OS**: uninstall the app. iOS and Android remove all app data on uninstall.

## Children

The app contains no user-generated content, no chat, no in-app purchases, and no ads. It is suitable for all ages and rated 4+.

## Changes to this policy

If anything ever changes (it is not expected to), this document will be updated and the change will be noted in the "Last updated" line above.
